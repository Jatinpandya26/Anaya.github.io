    • About Anaya

    ANAYA is an initiative whose idea is invoked by looking at the conditions of people during covid-19. Covid-19 has a frightful impact on the lives of daily wage workers and it's a do or die situation. Anaya provides a platform for people who want to serve their country with love, care and compassion. We connect you with the ones who are in need of food, cloth, education and shelter. You can help the needy people at your specified location.
